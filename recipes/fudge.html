Fudge

Ingredients:

  • 1 2/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2/3 cup undiluted evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup small marshmellows
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • any wanted toppings (sprinkles, m&m's, peppermint shavings, etc.)

Steps:

  1. In a saucepan on stovetop, combine sugar, butter, condensed milk
  2. Boil over medium heat until mixed constantly, about 5 min, stirring constantly
  3. Take off heat, add chocolate and marshmellows, beating mixture until mixed
  4. Add vanilla extract
  5. Add more chocolate / marshmellows until reached desired consistency and taste
  6. Add any additional ingredients wanted now
  7. Pour/spoon mixture into desired container (pan, cupcake tins, molds, etc.)
  8. Add any toppings, make sure to press into cooling fudge so it sticks
  9. Let cool to room temperature. Put into fridge if not being eaten that day
